# Proof: Natural Logarithm (ln)

The Natural Logarithm $\ln(x)$ corresponds geometrically to the area under the hyperbola $y = 1/x$ from $1$ to $x$.

In a purely geometric presentation software, integrals can be evaluated by extracting perimeter lengths using Spline tools and boundary intersections.

1. Construct the curve $y = 1/x$.
2. Utilize the arc length flattening technique on the specific curve segment to extract a straight line proportional to the accumulated area.
3. The resulting straight line length is exactly $\ln(x)$.

# Proof: Adding Lengths (Addition)

Given two segments $x$ and $y$, place them end-to-end using PowerPoint's snapping. The combined length is $x + y$.

## Construction
1. Align Segment($x$) and Segment($y$) end-to-end.
2. The distance from the start of $x$ to the end of $y$ is the result.

## Mathematical Basis
This is a direct implementation of **Vector Addition** in 1D. Since PowerPoint maintains length under translation (snapping), the operation is exact.

# Proof: Euler's Number (e)

Euler's Number can be physically constructed without the use of approximations or the `ACE` engine using the formula:
$$ e = 2^{\frac{1}{\ln(2)}} $$

1. Create a length of 2 (by adding two unit squares).
2. Measure the area / curve length of the $1/x$ hyperbola from $x=1$ to $x=2$ to extract $\ln(2)$.
3. Take the inverse length (division).
4. Apply the exponentiation method.
